Magic Mirror

MagicMirror is an open-source software that allows you to turn a display device, such as a TV or monitor, into a smart mirror. It can display a variety of information, such as the current time and date, weather forecast, news headlines, and more. MagicMirror is built using JavaScript. It is customizable, so you can add or remove features and change the layout and appearance of the mirror to suit your preferences.
